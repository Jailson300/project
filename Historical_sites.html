HISTORICAL SITES

Lai Haraoba

The Kangla, officially known as the Kangla Fort, is an old fortified palace at Imphal, in the Manipur state of India. It was formerly situated on both sides of the bank of the Imphal River, now remaining only on the western side in ruined conditions. Kangla means "the prominent part of the dry land" in old Meetei.Kangla Palace is one of Manipur's oldest and most historically significant buildings.
